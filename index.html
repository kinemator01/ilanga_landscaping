Our story

Founded on a love
for outdoor spaces.

Founded in 2024 by Nicholas Phali, Ilanga Landscaping started with a simple belief: every outdoor space deserves to be cared for properly. Nicholas holds a formal qualification in Landscaping, bringing real expertise — not just a lawnmower — to every job.

We work with homeowners, businesses, and warehouse operators across Cape Town to design, build, and care for outdoor spaces — from private gardens to industrial courtyards — that reflect nature, harmony, and real quality of life.

Whether it's a quick cleanup or a full transformation, we show up, do great work, and leave your space better than we found it.
